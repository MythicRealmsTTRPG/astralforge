Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Astral Forge collects, uses, and protects information when you visit this website, create an account, join a campaign, purchase services, or otherwise interact with the Astral Forge platform.

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Information We Collect

Depending on how you interact with Astral Forge, we may collect information such as:

  • Name and email address submitted through forms, account registration, or direct communication
  • Account information used for campaign participation, memberships, or access to tools and content
  • Payment-related information processed securely through third-party payment providers
  • Basic usage and analytics data, such as browser type, device information, pages visited, and session duration
  • Messages or information you voluntarily provide when contacting Astral Forge for support, collaboration, or campaign inquiries

How Information Is Used

Astral Forge may use collected information to:

  • Manage memberships, accounts, campaign bookings, and service access
  • Communicate regarding sessions, purchases, account support, updates, or inquiries
  • Deliver purchased products, software access, digital content, or community-related services
  • Improve website performance, user experience, and platform reliability
  • Protect the integrity and security of Astral Forge services

Payments

Payment transactions may be processed by third-party payment providers such as Stripe. Astral Forge does not store full payment card numbers or billing credentials on its own servers.

Third-Party Services

Astral Forge may integrate with external tools, platforms, and community services, including Root and other hosted providers. Your use of those services may also be governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

Cookies and Analytics

Astral Forge may use standard website technologies such as cookies, analytics tools, or session storage to support site functionality, understand usage patterns, and improve performance. These technologies generally help remember preferences, maintain sessions, and measure engagement.

Email Communication

If you contact Astral Forge, your email and related correspondence may be retained for purposes such as support, campaign communication, collaboration, scheduling, and account-related follow-up. Astral Forge does not sell personal information or share it for third-party advertising.

Data Retention

Personal information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, maintain business and legal records, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and support ongoing community or account administration.

Data Protection

Astral Forge uses reasonable technical and organizational safeguards intended to protect personal information. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

Astral Forge is not intended for children under the age of 13, and personal information is not knowingly collected from children under 13 without appropriate authorization where required by law.
